LinkedIn Engage helps you grow your professional network on LinkedIn with smart automation tools — all running locally in your browser.

FEATURES

Connection Automation
Build your network faster with tag-based search queries. Select Role, Industry, Market Focus, and Level tags to compose targeted LinkedIn searches. Choose from 6 note templates or write your own. Smart prioritization processes mutual connections first.

Feed Engagement
React and comment on LinkedIn feed posts with context-aware responses. The extension classifies posts into 13 categories (technical, achievement, humor, hiring, and more) and generates appropriate reactions and comments. Supports both English and Portuguese.

AI-Powered Comments (Optional)
Connect your own Groq API key for AI-generated comments that match the post's tone, language, and context. The AI analyzes the post text, existing comments, and reaction patterns to generate natural responses.

Company Follow
Search and follow companies in your target industry. Includes 60 curated mid-size companies as defaults with batch rotation for scheduled runs.

Dashboard & Analytics
Track your networking activity with a stats dashboard showing weekly totals, acceptance rates, reaction breakdowns, and a 14-day activity chart. Export your connection log as CSV.

Smart Safety Features
- Rate limit detection with automatic backoff
- Weekly invite limit guard (150 max)
- CAPTCHA and security challenge detection
- Duplicate post and profile tracking
- Human-like delays between actions

SCHEDULING
Set up recurring runs with Chrome's Alarms API. Configure intervals, query rotation, and batch sizes. The extension handles pagination, rate limits, and cooldowns automatically.

PRIVACY
All data stays on your device — no external servers, no tracking, no data collection. The only external call is the optional Groq AI integration using YOUR API key.

PERMISSIONS
- activeTab: Read LinkedIn pages for automation
- storage: Save your settings locally
- tabs: Manage automation tabs
- scripting: Inject automation into LinkedIn
- alarms: Schedule recurring runs
- notifications: Alert you on completion

IMPORTANT
This tool is for personal networking. Use responsibly and within LinkedIn's guidelines. Recommended: 50-100 actions per day with breaks between sessions.
