Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Data We Collect

LinkedIn Engage stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your automation preferences and settings
  • Profile URLs of connections you've sent invitations to (for deduplication)
  • Engagement analytics (post categories, reaction types, timestamps)
  • Nurture list entries (profile URLs, names, engagement counts)
  • Rate limiting counters

Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage via chrome.storage.local. No data is transmitted to any external server, API, or third-party service.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • LinkedIn credentials or session tokens
  • Message content or connection note text (templates are stored locally, never sent externally)
  • Browsing history outside of LinkedIn
  • Personal information beyond what you configure in the extension

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It communicates only with linkedin.com domains as required for its automation functionality.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Interact with the current LinkedIn tab
  • storage: Save your settings and engagement history locally
  • tabs: Open LinkedIn pages for automation
  • scripting: Inject automation scripts into LinkedIn pages
  • alarms: Schedule recurring automation runs
  • notifications: Alert you when automation completes or errors occur
  • host_permissions (linkedin.com): Required to interact with LinkedIn pages

Data Retention

  • Analytics log: capped at 5,000 entries
  • Sent profile URLs: capped at 2,000 entries
  • Nurture list: capped at 50 profiles, auto-cleaned after 10 days
  • Rate limit counters: auto-cleaned weekly

Your Control

You can clear all stored data at any time by:

  • Removing the extension from Chrome
  • Using Chrome's "Clear browsing data" for extensions
  • Using the extension's dashboard to reset settings

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be reflected in this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be noted in the extension's changelog.
